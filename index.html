Meet the Founder

Michael Tope Olaseigbe

Founder & Academic Director, MindTap Learning Hub

Michael Tope Olaseigbe is a graduate of Pure Chemistry from the Department of Chemistry, Faculty of Science, Ekiti State University, Ado-Ekiti, Nigeria.

With over six years of hands-on teaching experience, he has successfully prepared students for major external examinations including Common Entrance, WAEC, NECO, and JAMB, helping learners not only pass but excel with confidence.

His work is defined by patience, clarity, and measurable academic results, earning the trust of parents and students across different learning levels.

Teaching Philosophy

At MindTap Learning Hub, we believe that every child can learn when taught the right way. Our philosophy is centered on understanding — not cramming.

We break complex concepts into simple, relatable ideas, encourage questions, and build confidence step-by-step. Academic excellence is nurtured through consistency, encouragement, and structure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a session?

Each session typically lasts 1 hour for primary & secondary students, 90 minutes for university students.

Do you offer trial lessons?

Yes, we provide a free diagnostic session to assess learning needs and level.

What ages do you teach?

Students from primary school to university level.

Do you offer online and home lessons?

Yes, flexible options available for both online and in-home tutoring.

How are tutors vetted?

All tutors are thoroughly screened for qualifications, teaching experience, and effectiveness.
